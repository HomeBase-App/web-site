All You Need In 1 Page

Home Base is made for elderly and people with disabilities such as: Dementia, Alzheimer's, and Arthritis; to get information and access their websites as easily and quickly as possible.

By: Rishabh Baral, Rishikeshan Bharathi, Tejas Kamtam, and Dhanush Pulavarthy

Features designed for you:

Simple Structure and Accessibility

App Panel

Add and Delete Your Own websites to quickly access them with a SINGLE CLICK.

Widgets

Get live data for weather and news. Access your gmail, notes, reminders, and contacts on the home page itself.

Family Member Accessibility

Lets Family Memembers login to help the user with using this application.
